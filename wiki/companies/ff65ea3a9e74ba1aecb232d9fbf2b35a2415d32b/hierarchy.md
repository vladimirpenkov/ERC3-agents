# Company Hierarchy

Bellini Coatings uses a **traditional functional hierarchy** typical of a family-owned industrial company. The structure balances clear reporting lines with informal, cross-functional collaboration.

- [People, Departments & Roles](hr/people_and_roles.md)
- [Locations & Sites](company/locations_and_sites.md)

## Board & Ownership

### Chairman — Matteo Bellini

3rd generation family owner, semi-retired.  
Matteo provides strategic oversight and maintains family governance over long-term decisions.

### CEO — Giulia Bellini

4th generation family owner, active leader since 2020.  
Giulia drives the company's strategic direction, pushes for better use of data, and champions a more structured approach to innovation and customer collaboration.

## Executive Team

### COO — Operations & Supply Chain

Responsible for production across Italy and Serbia, logistics, and supply chain coordination.  
Oversees Plant Manager Italy, Plant Manager Serbia, Logistics & Supply Chain Manager, and Quality & HSE Manager.

### Head of Sales & Marketing

Owns the overall sales strategy, key account management, and marketing initiatives.  
Oversees Regional Sales Managers, Key Account Managers, Technical Sales / Application Specialists, and Marketing & Product Management.

### R&D Director

Leads research and development, technical service, and regulatory compliance.  
Oversees Lab Team Leaders, Chemists, Lab Technicians, Application Specialists, and Regulatory & Compliance.

### CFO — Finance & Administration

Manages accounting, controlling, treasury, credit management, and pricing administration.

### HR Director

Responsible for HR business partners, training & development, and payroll coordination across HQ and Serbia.

### IT & Digitalisation Manager

Owns the IT landscape including legacy Progress OpenEdge systems, infrastructure, and digital initiatives such as the chatbot.

## Department Heads / Senior Specialists

### Plant Manager Italy

Runs the flagship Italian production site focusing on high-value, complex formulations.  
Reports to COO.

### Plant Manager Serbia

Manages the Serbian factory handling standard and mid-complexity products.  
Reports to COO.

### Logistics & Supply Chain Manager

Balances capacity across plants, manages inventory, and coordinates deliveries.  
Reports to COO.

### Quality & HSE Manager

Defines quality standards, runs audits, manages complaints, and ensures safety/environmental compliance.  
Reports to COO.

### Regional Sales Managers

Lead sales in geographic clusters: Italy & Balkans, DACH, France/Benelux, Iberia.  
Report to Head of Sales & Marketing.

### R&D Team Leaders

Lead specialised lab teams (Solventborne, Waterborne, Floor Systems, etc.).  
Report to R&D Director.

## Reporting Lines (High-Level)

- **Board** (Chairman + external advisors) oversees strategic decisions.
- **CEO** reports to the Board and leads the Executive Team.
- **Executive Team** (COO, Head of Sales, R&D Director, CFO, HR Director, IT Manager) reports to CEO.
- **Department Heads** report to their respective executive sponsor.
- **Core team members** report to their department head or supervisor.

Every employee has exactly one **direct manager** recorded via the `reports_to` field in the employee registry.

The hierarchy respects formal reporting, but **day-to-day collaboration is informal and cross-functional**, especially between Sales, R&D, and Operations on customer projects.
