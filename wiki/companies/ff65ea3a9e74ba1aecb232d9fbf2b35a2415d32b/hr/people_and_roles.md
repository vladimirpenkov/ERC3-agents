# People, Departments & Roles

Bellini Coatings has about **150 employees** distributed across production, labs, sales, support and leadership roles.

## Main departments

The following department names are used consistently in the **employee registry**, time tracking and reporting:

- **Corporate Leadership**
- **Sales & Customer Success**
- **R&D and Technical Service**
- **Production – Italy**
- **Production – Serbia**
- **Logistics & Supply Chain**
- **Quality & HSE**
- **IT & Digital**
- **Human Resources (HR)**
- **Finance & Administration**

Each employee has exactly one **home department**, even if they collaborate with others.

## Examples of key roles

### Sales & Customer Success

- **Head of Sales & Marketing**
- **Regional Sales Manager**
- **Key Account Manager (KAM)**
- **Technical Sales / Application Specialist**
- **Customer Service / Inside Sales**

These roles own the **customer relationship**, manage opportunities in the CRM, and open projects for R&D and technical support.

### R&D and Technical Service

- **R&D Director**
- **Team Leaders (Solventborne, Waterborne, Floor Systems, etc.)**
- **Senior Chemist / Chemist**
- **Lab Technician**
- **Application Specialist / Technical Service Engineer**
- **Regulatory & Compliance Specialist**

They design formulations, run tests, document guidelines in the wiki and support customer trials.

### Production – Italy / Serbia

- **Plant Manager**
- **Production Supervisor (shift)**
- **Batch Operator / Mixer**
- **Filling and Packaging Operator**
- **Maintenance Technician**

Production roles ensure safe, efficient execution of recipes and cooperate with QA and logistics.

### Logistics & Supply Chain

- **Logistics & Supply Chain Manager**
- **Planner / Scheduler**
- **Warehouse Coordinator**
- **Shipping / Receiving Clerk**

This team balances capacity across plants, manages inventory and coordinates deliveries.

### Quality & HSE

- **Quality & HSE Manager**
- **Quality Engineer**
- **QA Technician**
- **HSE Specialist**

They define quality standards, run audits, manage complaints and ensure compliance with safety and environmental regulations.

### IT & Digital

- **IT & Digitalisation Manager**
- **Systems Administrator**
- **Progress OpenEdge Specialist**
- **Application Support Analyst**
- **Data & Reporting / BI Analyst**

IT keeps the **legacy systems** running and leads modernisation initiatives such as the chatbot and better reporting.

### HR

- **HR Director**
- **HR Generalist / Business Partner**
- **Recruiter (part‑time)**
- **Training & Development Specialist**
- **Payroll Coordinator**

HR manages hiring, employee records (including **skills and wills**), performance reviews and training.

### Finance & Administration

- **CFO**
- **Financial Controller**
- **Accountant**
- **Accounts Payable / Receivable Clerk**
- **Credit Manager**

Finance ensures accurate records, reporting and support for pricing and profitability analysis.

## Reporting and responsibilities

Each role comes with responsibilities for:

- Keeping data up to date (e.g. CRM, project registry, time tracking).
- Supporting cross‑functional collaboration (e.g. Sales ↔ R&D ↔ Production).
- Maintaining and improving documentation in the internal wiki.

The **skills & wills** model is used in development discussions: employees and managers jointly maintain profiles to reflect strengths and ambitions.
